=========================================

Summary of the NARA logs from the US Navy up to 1940 (`Record Group 24 <https://catalog.archives.gov/id/353>`_, `Series 581208 <https://catalog.archives.gov/id/581208>`_).

.. code-block:: bash

   to_csv.py --rg=24 --series=581208 --title
